RECORDING FEATURES
------------------

Local Recording:
Riverside records locally on each participant's device, not from the cloud. Video and audio are captured directly on the device and uploaded in the background. This means recording quality is completely independent of internet connection. Even if someone's WiFi drops, the local file is still perfect.

Video Quality:
Up to 4K (2160p) video on paid plans. 720p on the free plan. Constant frame rate recording.

Audio Quality:
48 kHz sample rate, 16-bit depth, uncompressed WAV files on paid plans. 44.1 kHz on the free plan.

Separate Tracks:
Every participant gets their own separate audio and video track. Screen share is captured as its own HD track. This makes editing easier because you can adjust one person without affecting the others.

Participants:
Up to 8 per recording session (1 host + 7 guests on video). Up to 1,000 live viewers on the Live plan. Up to 10,000 audience on Business plan for webinars.

Screen Sharing:
Any participant can share their screen. Captured as its own separate HD track.

Producer Role:
Full control of the session (camera switching, teleprompter, media board) without being recorded. Available on Business plan.

Media Board:
Upload and play audio or video files during the recording session.

Teleprompter:
Built-in with adjustable scroll speed, text size, and focus mode. Host-only on Pro. Host and guests on Business.


EDITING AND AI FEATURES
-----------------------

Text-Based Editor:
Edit video and audio by editing the transcript, like a Google Doc. Delete a sentence from the transcript and that section is cut from the video. Automatic multi-track sync. Search the transcript to find specific moments.

Magic Clips:
AI scans the full recording and automatically identifies the most engaging moments. Cuts them into 30-90 second clips optimized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Roughly 2 clips per 5 minutes of content.

Magic Audio:
One-click AI audio enhancement. Removes background noise, reduces echo, and balances audio levels across all speakers.

Co-Creator:
Conversational AI agent built into the editor. Chat with it to generate clips, thumbnails, newsletters, social posts, and other content from your recording. Example: "make a reel from the part about marketing" or "write a newsletter from this episode."

AI Transcription:
99% accuracy in 100+ languages with automatic speaker detection. Export as SRT, VTT, or plain text.

AI Captions and Subtitles:
Auto-generated in 100+ languages. Customizable styles, positioning, and animation.

AI Translation:
Translate transcripts and captions across languages.

Eye Contact Fix:
AI adjusts each speaker's eye gaze to appear as if looking directly at the camera.

Filler Word Removal:
One click removes all ums, uhs, and filler words.

Silence Removal:
Automatically cuts dead air from recordings.

AI Show Notes:
Auto-generated summary, key takeaways, and suggested episode title.

COMPETITOR COMPARISONS
----------------------

Riverside vs Zoom:
Zoom records from the cloud, so quality depends on internet. Riverside records locally at up to 4K, quality is always perfect. Riverside gives separate tracks per participant (Zoom puts everyone on one track). Riverside has built-in editing, transcription, and clip generation. Zoom recordings look and sound like meeting recordings. Riverside recordings look and sound professional.

Riverside vs Squadcast:
Both record locally for high-quality audio. Riverside adds 4K video, live streaming, and a full editing suite with AI tools. Squadcast is recording-only and now owned by Descript, so you need Descript for editing. Riverside is the all-in-one option.

Riverside vs Descript:
Descript started as an editor and added recording. Riverside started as a recorder and built editing in. Riverside has stronger recording quality with true local 4K capture. Both offer text-based editing. Descript has more advanced post-production (voice cloning). Riverside has a cleaner, more focused workflow.

Riverside vs StreamYard:
StreamYard focuses on live streaming with cloud-dependent quality. Riverside does both recording and live streaming with 4K local capture. Riverside has built-in editing and AI tools that StreamYard does not offer.

Riverside vs Zencastr:
Riverside has superior video quality (4K vs lower resolution) and a more comprehensive feature set including live streaming, AI editing, and clip generation. Zencastr is slightly cheaper and simpler but less complete.
